Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need internet to use this platform?

No! Our app works offline. Once you download the content, you can access lessons, notes, and quizzes without internet.

How can students ask doubts and teachers receive them?

Students can submit their doubts easily using the Contact Us option within the platform. All queries are automatically sent to the registered teacher’s email, ensuring timely guidance and personalized support.

Is the content available in local languages?

Yes. Students can learn in Punjabi, Hindi, or English, making it easier for rural learners to understand concepts.

Can this platform run on low-end devices?

Absolutely. The platform is optimized for entry-level smartphones and computers, ensuring smooth access even with poor connectivity.

Is this platform free for rural schools?

Yes, it is designed to support government schools and rural communities without extra cost.

Is the app safe for children?

Yes. The app is 100% safe, free from ads, and designed only for educational purposes.

How will this app help in exam preparation??

The platform includes guided notes, practice papers, mock tests, and previous year question banks.

What subjects are available for school students?

We provide interactive lessons in Math, Science, English, and Social Studies for classes 1–12, plus skill-based courses like English speaking and coding.
